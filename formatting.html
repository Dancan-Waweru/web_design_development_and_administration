Users fo HTML are sometimes suprised to find that HTML gives them little control over the way that a page is displayed.
It should be remembered that HTML was developed as a means of marking up the structures of a document not as a way of determining its presentation.
Formatting text to appear on a a web page is therefore different from formatting text to appear in a printed document
